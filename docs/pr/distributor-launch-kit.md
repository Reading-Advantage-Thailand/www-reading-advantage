## 1. THE STRATEGY: WHY YOU CAN SELL THIS WITH CONFIDENCE

You are not selling "new software." You are selling **Guaranteed English Department Stability.**

Schools are terrified of three things:
1.  Foreign teachers quitting mid-term.
2.  Thai teachers feeling overwhelmed by English programs.
3.  Parents complaining about low quality.

**We solve all three.**
*   **If they lack teachers:** We provide the **Managed Service** (We put our certified facilitators in the room).
*   **If they have teachers:** We provide the **Blended Learning Program** (Physical workbooks + digital app) + **The "Big 4" Protocol** (We train their teachers to be facilitators).

## 4. OBJECTION BATTLE CARD (Handling "Gun Shy" Moments)

### Objection 1: "Our Thai teachers aren't good enough at English to teach this."
*   **The Confidence Response:** *"That is exactly why we built this. The AI handles the 'Instruction' (perfect pronunciation, grammar explanations). Your teacher handles the 'Motivation' and 'Classroom Management.' We have Grade 3 teachers successfully running this right now with just 2 days of training."*

### Objection 2: "We bought software before, and nobody used it."
*   **The Confidence Response:** *"That’s the difference. We don't just give you software; we give you an **Implementation Manager**. We provide quarterly 'Fidelity Reports' (show them the Observation Form). If a teacher stops using it, we know within 24 hours and we intervene. We don't let you fail."*

### Objection 3: "It's too expensive compared to textbooks."
*   **The Confidence Response:** *"Textbooks don't grade themselves. Textbooks don't plan the lesson. Textbooks don't speak with a native accent. This replaces the textbook, the workbook, the grading time, AND the need for an expensive foreign teacher assistant. It's actually cheaper than your current 'total cost' of English."*
