**One Integrated System: Workbooks + App**

Our **Blended Learning Program** combines physical workbooks with our AI-powered extensive reading app into a single, aligned curriculum. No parallel systems. No curriculum gaps. Just consistent, research-backed English instruction.

*   **Workbook-First Rigor:** Deep cognitive engagement before AI assistance.

## THE RESEARCH
**Evidence-Backed Methodology**

A controlled one-year study (Aka, 2019) found that extensive reading instruction (2 hours/week) outperformed grammar instruction on reading test scores. On the post-test reading section (0-100 scale), the extensive reading group scored higher (61.78 vs 52.29; ~+9.5 points).

**Our approach:**
*   2 periods/week of research-based extensive reading (Aka 2019)
*   3 periods/week of integrated workbook instruction for curriculum alignment
*   Transparent per-school reporting: pre/post benchmarks, reading volume, fidelity scores
