### Position: School Lead Generation Officer

**Company:** Reading Advantage
**Location:** Khon Kaen area (expandable)
**Type:** Full-time

#### Compensation
- Base Salary: 7,500 THB/month
- Commission: 350 THB per confirmed school assembly booking
- Potential earnings: 14,500 - 21,500+ THB/month based on performance

#### Responsibilities
- Cold call private schools to schedule free English reading activities
- Coordinate with academic departments and English department heads
- Send information and follow up on contacts
- Maintain accurate records in tracking system
- Start with Khon Kaen area schools, expand to other regions

#### Requirements
- Thai national with clear speaking voice
- Confident on the phone
- Basic computer skills (Excel, Email)
- Persistent, not easily discouraged
- Punctual and responsible

#### Nice to Have
- Previous telemarketing or call center experience
- Experience with schools or education sector
- Basic English skills
- Located in Khon Kaen or Isan region

#### Growth Path
This role is designed to develop into a full Sales & Marketing position:
1. **Month 1-3:** Lead Generation (current role)
2. **Month 4-6:** Add demo visit participation
3. **Month 7-12:** Full Sales Executive with higher base + commission
4. **Year 2+:** Marketing Coordinator or Sales Manager potential

#### Why This Role Exists
- We have no sales team currently
- Need systematic outreach to schools
- Assembly program requires high-volume bookings
- This person will be foundational to building sales function

## INTERVIEW PROCESS

### Phone Screen (10 minutes)
1. Confirm basic qualifications
2. Assess voice quality and confidence
3. Ask about availability
4. Schedule in-person or video interview

### Interview Questions

**Opening:**
- "เล่าเกี่ยวกับตัวเองให้ฟังหน่อย" (Tell me about yourself)
- "ทำไมสนใจตำแหน่งนี้?" (Why are you interested in this position?)

**Experience:**
- "เคยโทรติดต่อลูกค้าไหม? เป็นอย่างไรบ้าง?" (Have you called customers before? How was it?)
- "เวลาถูกปฏิเสธทำอย่างไร?" (What do you do when rejected?)

**Role Play:**
- "สมมติว่าโทรไปโรงเรียน ลองพูดให้ฟังหน่อย" (Pretend you're calling a school, show me)
- Assess: Voice clarity, confidence, friendliness

**Practical:**
- "ใช้ Excel เป็นไหม? ทำอะไรได้บ้าง?" (Can you use Excel? What can you do?)
- "ทำงานเริ่ม 9 โมงได้ไหม?" (Can you start work at 9 AM?)

**Close:**
- "มีคำถามอะไรไหม?" (Do you have any questions?)
- "เมื่อไหร่เริ่มงานได้?" (When can you start?)

### Evaluation Criteria

| Criteria | Weight | What to Look For |
|----------|--------|------------------|
| Voice Quality | 25% | Clear, friendly, confident |
| Persistence | 25% | Doesn't give up easily |
| Basic Skills | 20% | Computer, organization |
| Attitude | 20% | Positive, eager to learn |
| Availability | 10% | Can start soon, reliable schedule |

## ONBOARDING PLAN

### Day 1: Orientation
- Company overview and mission
- Product introduction (show the app)
- Team introductions
- Office setup (computer, phone, email)

### Day 2: Training
- 2 hours: Product deep-dive
- 2 hours: Script practice and role-play
- 1 hour: Spreadsheet training
- End of day: Practice calls (internal)

### Week 1: Supervised Calling
- Supervisor listens to all calls
- Immediate feedback
- Adjust script as needed
- Target: 30 calls/day (learning mode)

### Week 2: Increasing Independence
- Supervisor spot-checks calls
- Daily debrief meetings
- Target: 40 calls/day
- First bookings expected

### Week 3-4: Full Production
- Target: 50-60 calls/day
- Weekly coaching sessions
- Review KPIs together
- Celebrate first commission!
